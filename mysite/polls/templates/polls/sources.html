Resources for handling stress:


  • Government: Mental Health - coping with stress
    • Government of Canada official website provide clear information about the background of stress, health effects of stress and strategies to confront stress.

  • CMHA National- Stress
    • This brochure from the Canadian Mental Health Association(CMHA) provide detailed steps to overcome stress, as well as ways to prevent stress.

  • Canadian Medical Association- Stress
    • Canadian Medical Association (CMA) provide list of stress causes and identified 4 levels of stress we might experience during COVID-19.

  • Anxiety Canada
    • Anxiety Canada provides self-help information, community programs and workshops for those who feel they need it.

  • Stress Strategies- Resources
    • Stress Strategies provide a comprehensive list of resources for outcomes of stress, such as substance abuse and mental health.
