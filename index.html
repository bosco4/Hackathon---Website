3 Types of Stress

There are 3 main types of stress as indicated down below.

Learn More >

Acute Stress

Acute stress is the body's immediate reaction to a new and challenging situation.

Chronic Stress

When you have high stress levels for an extended period of time, you have chronic stress.

Episodic Acute Stress

Episodic acute stress is when you have frequent episodes of acute stress.
